# Foundations of quantum computing

The **Foundations** track on IBM Quantum Learning is a series of graduate‑level style courses that introduce the theoretical
and practical basis of quantum information and quantum computation. It is designed for students and professionals in
computer science, physics, engineering, and mathematics who want a rigorous but accessible entry point to quantum computing.

Read more at [IBM Quantum Learning](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/learning/en/courses).
